

# Bliss Photos - Photography Studio Website

A beautiful, single-page scrolling website for your newborn, kids & maternity photography studio with a warm blush & cream aesthetic.

---

## Design System

**Color Palette:**
- **Primary**: Soft blush pink (#F5E6E0)
- **Accent**: Warm cream/ivory (#FDF8F5)
- **Text**: Warm charcoal (#4A4A4A)
- **Highlights**: Rose gold accents
- **Ample white space throughout**

**Typography:**
- Elegant serif headings (warm, motherly feel)
- Clean sans-serif body text (modern readability)
- Calm, friendly, and premium
